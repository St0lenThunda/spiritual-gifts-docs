# Tier Breakdown

We offer three tiers designed to match the maturity and size of your ministry.

| Feature | **Starter** | **Growth** | **Enterprise** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Best For** | Small church plants or single ministries | Established churches with multiple leaders | Networks, denominations, or multi-site churches |
| **Assessment Access** | Limited (monthly cap) | Higher limits | **Unlimited** |
| **History Retention** | 30–90 Days | Full History | Full History |
| **Administrator Seats** | 1 Admin | 3–5 Admins | Unlimited |
| **Reporting** | Basic PDF | Enhanced PDF | **Branded PDF** |
| **Support** | Email | Priority Email | White-glove / Phone |
| **Organization** | Single Org | Single Org | Multi-Org Support |
| **Member Approval** | Basic (Manual) | Advanced (Bulk Actions) | Full Governance |
| **Customization** | Standard | Standard | Custom Gift Weighting |

---

> [!tip] **Philosophy of Tiers**
> We do not gate *theology* behind a paywall. Every tier gets the full, accurate spiritual gift assessment. Higher tiers unlock **organizational capacity**, not spiritual truth.
