## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### **What are spiritual gifts?**
Spiritual gifts are special abilities given by the Holy Spirit to believers for building up the church and serving others. The Bible describes these gifts in passages like Romans 12:6-8, 1 Corinthians 12:4-11, and Ephesians 4:11-13.

### **How accurate is this assessment?**
Our assessment is based on established questionnaire methodologies used in churches worldwide for decades. While no test can perfectly measure spiritual reality, this tool provides a helpful starting point for self-reflection and conversation with spiritual mentors.

### **How long does the assessment take?**
Most people complete the assessment in **8-12 minutes**. There's no time limit, so users can take breaks and return later.

### **Can someone take the assessment multiple times?**
Yes! We encourage retaking the assessment periodically (every 6-12 months) to see how gifts develop over time. The dashboard tracks all previous results.

### **Is this appropriate for youth groups?**
Absolutely. The questions are written at a reading level accessible to teens and adults. Youth leaders have used this tool successfully for confirmation classes and youth ministry training.

### **What about data privacy?**
We take privacy seriously:
- No passwords stored (magic-link auth)
- Email addresses are masked in logs
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Users can delete their data anytime

### **Can we customize the questions or gifts?**
The current version uses a standardized question set. Custom question sets and additional gift categories can be discussed for enterprise deployments.

### **Do you offer bulk/church pricing?**
Yes! We offer special pricing for churches and ministries. Contact us for group rates.

### **Is there a free trial?**
Yes, individuals can take the assessment for free. Church-wide deployments include a 30-day trial period.

### **What technology is this built on?**
- **Frontend**: Vue.js 3 with modern reactive design
- **Backend**: FastAPI (Python) with PostgreSQL database
- **Hosting**: Cloud-native, scalable infrastructure
- **Security**: Enterprise-grade with 100% test coverage
